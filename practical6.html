Tribute to MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, known as MS Dhoni, is one of India's most legendary cricketers. As a former captain of the Indian cricket team, he led India to victories in the ICC T20 World Cup (2007), ICC Cricket World Cup (2011), and ICC Champions Trophy (2013). Dhoni is known for his calm demeanor, excellent wicket-keeping skills, and finishing abilities.

Career Stats

Matches: 350+ (ODIs), 90+ (Tests), 98 (T20Is)

Runs: 10,000+ (ODIs), 4,800+ (Tests), 1,600+ (T20Is)

Centuries: 10 (ODIs), 6 (Tests)

Notable Achievements: 3 ICC Trophies, IPL titles with CSK
